Our Story

SafeLife was established on 26th December 2022, with a mission to promote health awareness, well-being, and lifestyle improvements among students and the community. Founded by a group of passionate individuals, SafeLife has reached numerous milestones, including conducting health drives, outreach activities, and workshops that positively impact lives.
